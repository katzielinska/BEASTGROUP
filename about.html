About us

Suits and Sandals isn’t just a small indie game company. We started a community of game enthusiats sharing our experiences, opnions and hobbies and it grew to become a company.

Today we aim to help small creators build and promote their first games, while we keep our tight community at the side.

If you’re a creator looking for like minded people, you should check out our discord group, we’re always online and hanging out.
